# Introduction to Generative AI (Coursera)

## Project Summary

*Introduction to Generative AI* is an online course hosted on Coursera that introduces learners to the fundamentals of generative artificial intelligence. The course is designed for a broad audience and focuses on building conceptual understanding and confidence rather than technical depth or coding expertise.

Within the BOBPE ecosystem, this course serves as a public-facing entry point for AI literacy and foundational understanding.

---

## Course Focus

The course emphasizes:
- What generative AI is and how it works at a high level
- Common capabilities and limitations of modern AI systems
- Appropriate and inappropriate use cases
- Practical examples drawn from real-world applications
- Clear, accessible explanations for non-specialists

The content is designed to demystify AI and provide learners with a mental model they can use to engage critically with AI tools.

---

## Audience

- Students exploring AI for the first time
- Educators and professionals seeking foundational literacy
- Parents and community members interested in understanding AI
- Lifelong learners without a technical background

No prior experience with AI or programming is required.
